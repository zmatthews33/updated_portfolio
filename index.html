About Me

My name is Zach Matthews and I'm a full stack developer. I recently completed Vanderbilt's Full Stack Web Development Program. I have a Bachelors' Degree in Music and Entertainment Business and another in Event Production from the University of Memphis. Whether it be music, tv/film sets or furniture, I have spent most of my life creating things. Through years of playing drums in a group, creating in the studio or and leading a crew on set, I have been forced to develop some serious problem solving skills and the ability to learn and adapt on the fly. These things have led me to the world of programming. I look forward to a long career of learning, creating and solving problems (in no particular order).
